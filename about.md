## 👨‍💻 **Professional Summary**
Cyber Security Engineer with hands-on experience across **Threat Intelligence, SOC Operations**, and **Security Automation**. Skilled in **threat research, malware analysis, SIEM/EDR engineering, and cloud-based infrastructure**, combining **OSINT** and **closed-source intelligence** to produce actionable insights, automate workflows, and strengthen proactive defence capabilities.

---

### **Key Responsibilities**
- 📊 **SIEM & EDR Engineering:** Deploy, onboard, configure, and optimise **SIEM** and **EDR** solutions for new clients, ensuring effective log ingestion, telemetry, and detection coverage.
- 🔍 **Threat Research:** Investigate emerging cyber threats using **OSINT** and **non-public intelligence sources** to deliver actionable insights.  
- 🛡️ **SOC Operations:** Support Security Operations Centres through **incident triage**, **threat hunting**, **detection engineering**, and the development of actionable detection content.
- 🧠 **Malware Analysis:** Perform **static, dynamic, and automated analysis** to identify IOCs, threat behaviors, and adversary tactics.  
- ☁️ **Infrastructure Management:** Deploy and maintain **cloud infrastructure** supporting core threat intelligence platforms such as **OpenCTI** and **KASM Workspaces**.  
- 🤖 **Security Automation:** Design and implement automated workflows using **SOAR platforms**, and **Python** to streamline investigations, enrich alerts, and improve operational efficiency.  
- 🛡️ **SOC Enablement:** Provide **detection rules**, **hunt packages**, and intelligence reports to strengthen **SOC operations** and proactive threat hunting.
